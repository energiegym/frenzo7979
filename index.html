What is Reeach?

Reeach is India’s own smart, affordable, and reliable taxi booking platform – made for the way we travel. From daily office commutes to airport drops and outstation rides, Reeach connects you with trusted drivers in just a few taps. No surge pricing, no confusion – just smooth, safe, and budget-friendly travel every single time.

With features like live ride tracking, voice booking, secure payments, and 24/7 support, Reeach is built to make cab travel easier and more comfortable for everyone — from metro cities to small towns across India.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have doubts? Get answers right here

How do I book a ride with Reeach?

Simply download our app, enter your pickup and destination locations, choose your preferred ride type, and confirm your booking. A driver will be assigned to you within minutes.

Is Reeach available 24/7?

Yes, Reeach operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Our drivers are available round the clock to serve you whenever you need a ride.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), and cash payments. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I track my ride in real-time?

Our app provides real-time GPS tracking so you can see your driver's location and estimated arrival time throughout your journey.

How do I become a Reeach driver?

To become a driver, download our driver app, complete the registration process, submit required documents, and pass our background check. Once approved, you can start earning immediately.

What safety measures does Reeach have?

We conduct thorough background checks on all drivers, provide real-time ride tracking, emergency assistance button, driver and vehicle verification, and 24/7 customer support for safety concerns.
